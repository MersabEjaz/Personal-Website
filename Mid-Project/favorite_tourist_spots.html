Favourit Tourist spots

Swat-Kalam

The Switzerland of Pakistan is the major tourist attraction. It is one of the oldest civilization. It is located in foothills of Hindukush Mountain range. The Major attraction of Swat valley includes its archeology, the lush green sites, the history and their art.

Hunza-Skardu

Hunza, known as Heaven on Earth, is a beautiful mountainous valley located in the Gilgit Baltistan region of Pakistan. It is surrounded by Rakaposhi, Hunza peak, BojahagurDuanasir 2, Darmyani Peak, Ghenta Sar, Ultar Sar and Ladyfinger Peak. Baltit and Altit fort are attraction for tourist.

Murree

The most popular hill station of Pakistan is Murree. It was built according to building frame of European cities with Church in the center. Mall road is running along with commercial places and administrative offices. Murree is located betwwn Kashmir Point and Pindi Point.
